Uses **Swap Charges** (no spell slots)
Charges: Lv1–2: **2** | Lv3–5: **3** | Lv6–9: **4** | Lv10+: **5**
Restore on **long rest**
Requires **INT check**
Unwilling targets contest (INT/STR, DM)
Range: **50m**, line of sight
Needs **2 targets**
No partial swaps
Bigger/magical targets → higher DC
On fail: **no Swap until end of next turn**

#### **Lv1:**

**Swap:** swap 2 items, no combat
**Cost:** 1 charge

#### **Lv3:**

**Swap:** usable in combat
**Cost:** 1 charge
**Cd:** 2 rounds

**Team Swap:** prep swap for ally (they use reaction + INT roll)
**Cost:** 2 charges
**Cd:** 1 short rest

#### **Lv5:**

**Mark Swap:** mark 1 item (INT)

Item ↔ item: any range
Self ↔ mark: 50m

**Cost:** mark 1 | item swap 1 | self swap 2
**Cd:** self swap 1 short rest
**Limit:** 1 mark

#### **Lv6:**

**Quick Swap:** arm’s reach, uses DEX, bonus action
**Cost:** 1 charge
**Cd:** 1 round

#### **Lv8:**

**Picture Swap:** draw item (1 min), swaps with closest match
**Cost:** 3 charges
**No combat / no creatures**

#### **Lv10:**

**Advanced Swap:** can swap creatures & spell effects (not Mark Swap)

Unwilling targets: advantage
Strong targets: higher DC

**Cost:** 2 charges
**Cd:** 1 short rest
